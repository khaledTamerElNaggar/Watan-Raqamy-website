About ECC-AI :

As part of IEEEâ€™s role as the world's first non-profit scientific and research organization, we are pleased to organize the Egyptian Consultants Congress of Artificial Intelligence (ECC-AI 2024), scheduled to be held at the German University in New Cairo on Thursday, December 19, 2024, at 1:00 PM. This congress aims to discuss the latest developments in artificial intelligence (AI) and its diverse applications across various fields, including cybersecurity, healthcare, education, smart cities, and other vital sectors. The event will feature the participation of a distinguished group of government officials, experts, researchers, and representatives of leading companies in this field. The congress will include various activities, such as keynote speeches on the latest advancements in AI and its applications across different sectors, as well as its role in driving development on both national and regional levels. Additionally, the congress will host several workshops and brainstorming sessions to discuss the proposed roadmap for AI applications, providing a platform for exchanging ideas and experiences among researchers, decision-makers, and leaders from government, industry, and academia, ultimately leading to actionable recommendations for the future. About IEEE Consultants Network :

About IEEE Consultants Network:The mission of the IEEE Egypt Consultants Network is to establish a dynamic and supportive network of independent technical and engineering consultants in Egypt. The Egypt Consultants Network aims to provide a platform that fosters professional development, networking, and collaboration among consultants, academia, industry, and government. The mission includes promoting the use of independent consultants by businesses and industries, enhancing the visibility and credibility of consultants, and facilitating knowledge-sharing and innovation in the consulting field. The Egypt Consultants Network STRIVEs to empower its members with resources, opportunities, and connections that contribute to their individual success and collectively elevate the consulting profession in Egypt.
