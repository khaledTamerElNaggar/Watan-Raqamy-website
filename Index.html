About ECC-AI :

As part of IEEEâ€™s role as the world's first non-profit scientific and research organization, we are pleased to organize the Egyptian Consultants Congress of Artificial Intelligence (ECC-AI 2024). This congress aims to discuss the latest developments in artificial intelligence (AI) and its diverse applications across various fields, including cybersecurity, healthcare, education, smart cities, and other vital sectors. The event will feature the participation of a distinguished group of government officials, experts, researchers, and representatives of leading companies in this field. the congress will host several workshops and brainstorming sessions to discuss the proposed roadmap for AI applications, providing a platform for exchanging ideas and experiences among researchers, decision-makers, and leaders from government, industry, and academia, ultimately leading to actionable recommendations for the future. About IEEE Consultants Network :

The mission of the IEEE Egypt Consultants Network is to establish a dynamic and supportive network of independent technical and engineering consultants in Egypt. We aim to provide a platform that fosters professional development, networking, and collaboration among consultants, academia, industry, and government. We STRIVEs to empower our members with resources, opportunities, and connections that contribute to their individual success and collectively elevate the consulting profession in Egypt.
